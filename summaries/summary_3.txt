The story begins in the continent of Aradia, ruled by a council of powerful mages. The council has received reports of a great evil spreading throughout the land, destroying villages and corrupting the natural order. Ruthuvikas the Druid, Gopal the Cleric, and Mahek the Fighter are on a quest to put an end to the evil. They find the source of the evil in a group of powerful necromancers, who have been raising an army of undead creatures to conquer Aradia. They use their skills and powers to infiltrate the Necromancers' stronghold, fighting their way through undead minions and dangerous traps. Eventually, they face off against the necromancing themselves. They defeat them, but the evil is manipulated by a powerful demon, Baelzebub, and they have to fight to stop him from achieving his goal. The heroes are hailed as champions by the people of the continent and awarded with titles and honors. They set out on new quests, seeking to rid the world of other evils.
